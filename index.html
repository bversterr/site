About Us

Built for business.
Uncompromising on security.

Blue Ghost was founded in 2005 by a team of senior security engineers and threat analysts with backgrounds across financial services, critical infrastructure, and enterprise IT. We specialise in proactive cyber defence — identifying and closing vulnerabilities before they become incidents.

From securing email infrastructure and hardening corporate networks to 24/7 threat monitoring and rapid incident response — we work as an extension of your security team, tailored to the scale and risk profile of your organisation.

Our Core Services

// Email Security

Email
Security

Comprehensive email threat protection covering phishing, business email compromise, malware attachments, and spoofed senders. Includes SPF, DKIM and DMARC enforcement, real-time scanning, and automated quarantine — fully integrated with Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace.

// Domain Security

Domain
Protection

Monitor and protect your brand's digital identity. We detect typosquatted domains, enforce DMARC at scale, harden DNS infrastructure, and alert you to dark-web credential exposure — keeping impersonators and phishing domains from reaching your customers.

Network
